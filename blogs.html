    • WHY GOOD CINEMA SHAPES YOUR THINKING

      • Watching films is often cosnsidered as a means of entertainment , which it obviously is but i think cinema has a bigger role to play in one's life. Films can really change the way you think about things. Personally i rememeber when i first started watching films i must have been 15-16. But i truly started exploring films when i was about 17-18. I explored all genres and realised how different stories can completly change your worldview. One such film is DEAD POETS SOCIETY, this film is special because this was one of the earliest films that deeply affected me. The film talks about how you should not see the world, how art, romance, literature are the things we stay alive for. I think the films we watch, the literature we consume, the people we listen to online and in real life shapes you as a person and one should chosse carefully what he/she is consuming.I think if someone is just starting off with watching films they can first watch the top 100 imdb films . Then see what genre film really excites them and watch the best films from around the world , watching the film from a different part of the world tells you a lot about the way people life their , how they think etc.
